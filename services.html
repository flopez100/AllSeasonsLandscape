Services

There is a reason why we named ourselves All Seasons Landscape. From spring to winter, we are here to help you every step of the way

Lawn Care

Lawn care is important for several reasons, and it plays a significant role in maintaining healthy, attractive, and functional outdoor spaces. Here's how we help you make the most of your outdoor spaces:

  • Lawn mowing: Regular lawn mowing and trimming to maintain an appropriate grass height.
  • Edging: Creating clean and defined borders around lawns and walkways.
  • Fertilization: Applying fertilizers to promote healthy grass growth.
  • Weed Control: Managing and preventing the growth of weeds in the lawn.
  • Removing debris: Clearing away leaves, branches, and other debris that has accumulated.

Tree & Hedge Care

Tree and hedge care are essential components of landscaping and property maintenance. We conduct this service with consideration for the specific needs of different tree and shrub species, local climate conditions, and the desired aesthetic and functional goals of each customer.

  • Tree and Shrub Pruning: Trimming overgrown branches to promote health and shape.
  • Hedge Trimming: Shaping and maintaining hedges for a neat appearance.
  • Planting: Adding new trees, shrubs, flowers, and other plants to the landscape.
  • Mulching: Applying mulch to flower beds and around trees to conserve moisture and deter weeds.

Power Washing

  • House Power Washing: Cleaning the exterior of houses, including siding, brick, stucco, and more to remove dirt, grime, mold, and mildew.
  • Deck and Patio Power Washing: Cleaning wooden or composite decks, patios, and outdoor furniture to remove stains, algae, and other contaminants.
  • Driveway Power Washing: Cleaning concrete, asphalt, or gravel driveways to remove oil stains, dirt, and grime.
  • Sidewalk and Walkway Power Washing: Cleaning concrete or stone sidewalks and walkways to remove dirt, moss, algae, and other debris.
  • Fence Power Washing: Cleaning wooden, vinyl, or metal fences to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and stains.

Snow Removal

  • Snow Removal: Snow plowing, shoveling, and snow blowing services to clear driveways, walkways, parking lots, and other outdoor areas.
  • Deicing and Salting: Applying ice melt or salt to prevent ice buildup on pathways, driveways, and stairs is crucial for safety.
